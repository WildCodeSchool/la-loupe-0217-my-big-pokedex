How does it work?

Create your account

Ever wanted an easy app where you can add, organize, sort (and more) all the Pokémon you got? My Tiny Pokédex is here! So don't waste more time and create your free account now!

Add all your Pokémon games!

From Pokémon Yellow to Black or Moon, all games are available! All you have to do is going to your profile, browse all the Pokémon games and add those you own to your account.

Catch them all!

It's time to catch them all now! Go to your Pokédex, add the Pokémon you caught in your games, check their profile, update their level... everything is possible!
